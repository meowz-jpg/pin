How to Apply Sunscreen Correctly

Many people don't apply enough sunscreen.

Use the two-finger rule:

  • Apply two finger lengths of sunscreen to your face
  • Apply 15 minutes before sun exposure
  • Reapply every 2–3 hours

Also remember to apply sunscreen on your neck, ears, and hands.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need sunscreen every day?

Yes. Even on cloudy days, UV rays can still damage your skin.

Is SPF 30 enough?

SPF 30 blocks about 97% of UVB rays, while SPF 50 blocks about 98%.

Can sunscreen replace moisturizer?

Some hydrating sunscreens can act as both moisturizer and SPF.

Using sunscreen daily is the best way to protect your skin and keep it healthy and glowing.
